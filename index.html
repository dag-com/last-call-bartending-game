      How to play

      1. New here? Start with 📚 Training — a guided lesson that walks you through making your first cocktail.
      2. Read the customer order at the top.
      3. Work through the steps like a bartender: glass → pour → mix → garnish.
      4. Add ingredients and dial in each amount in ml (some use dashes/leaves). Watch them pour in.
      5. Pick a method and a garnish, then Serve.
      6. Earn up to 3 stars per drink. Pours within a small tolerance still count!
      7. Basic auto-picks the glass & method; Advanced leaves it all to you; Mixologist is a free-pour sandbox.
      8. Try 🔥 Endless Shift — serve random orders for points until you run out of lives. Peek at the Recipe Book anytime.
